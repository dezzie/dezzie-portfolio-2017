Hello! My name is Dezzie.

I work for IBM Watson now! Specifically, on the Watson Developer Cloud, designing experiences that are meant to be taken apart. Our work enables developers and businesses to build using Watson, specifically with technologies that detect and interpret emotion, personality, attitudes, values, or communication styles. Good design in this space gives people the clarity to solve problems from points of view that were not possible before, but are innately human.

Some, but not all, of my work is NDA, and I finally feel like I'm starting to form my own point of view as a designer. Soon I hope to share with you all the stories and process behind what the team of crazy sharp people I work with are doing.
