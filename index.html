Hello!
My name is Dezzie.

I work on the developer experience for IBM Watson. It's my job to make it simple for people to build things using current artificial intelligence technology. Strong design in this space gives people the clarity to solve problems from new, more human, points of view.

Sometimes I speak at conferences, but I prefer to teach.

There have been no updates to this portfolio since I joined the team because let's face it, we designers mostly do that stuff when we're lookin' ;)
